Centre of Excellence

OpenSource Technologies

Introduction:

A department within an organization is known for the strength of its staff members, the quality work they do show their expertise and team work, the milestone they achieve over a period of time, the followers they make within the organizations so on.. In academic emvironment, each department should strive for and should have some unique identity which is well appreciated and recognized by the people within the organization as well as outsiders. The idea of “Centre of Excellence” gives branding to a particular institute or department. The COE is a medium to exhibit or showcase the strength or expertise in one or more area of interest or something which fetches you recognition & revenue. An opprotunity well recognized indentity by the outsiders.


Objective:

The objectives behind setting up a COE in IT department are as follows:

  • Shifting the focus from proprietery software to open source software used in executing various academic activities.
  • To achieving expertise & providing services in OSS domain.
  • To develop faculty & student community who will contrribute towards further development of OSS.
  • To indetify the scope to carry out research oriented project

Motivation:

As a policy, DBIT has always been a strong supporter of open source software and kept emphaziziing on use of OSS for conducting practical and implementing projects. The only challenge was , the lack of awareness among the staff members about the availability of various useful OSS tools/softwares. The IT department has been contributing in one or the other way towards the overall growth of the institute as far as use of the technology is concerned e.g. Moodle Administration, Moodle Training, Software development etc. As a mission of the institute & IT department, we plan to take it to the next level and create an identity for ourselves among various technical institutions and in turn within OSS community.

Mission:

Mastering all OSS tools/softwares falling under identified streams To be able to contribute towards the OSS community by promoting , developing and customizing OSS tools/softwares. To develop a culture wherein everyone wants to know more & explore OSS To associate with universities/institutes/industry who are into OSS development & its application. To be known as OSS experts in terms of training, R&D projects, consultancy, software etc

Beneficiaries/Stakeholder:

Mainly there are four beneficiaries:
Student: Studens will be involved in COE activites as a developer keeping in mind the long term goals. COE members will be responsible for making students aware of the benefits of OSS compared with proprietory softwares (Cost & Licensing issues). Tag Line : Learn , Master , Share and Contribute towards OSS.
Faculty: COE will give an oportunity to explore beyond the subject syllabus so that one can contribute towards the growth of the department. Practical exposure to various OSS will definetly boost the confidence. Project implementation through open source plateform one of the key factors. Sense of serving in a different way apart from teaching.
Department: Gradually department will be known for its expertise , services and quality projects thus creating an unique identity within and outside the campus.
Organization: Outside the campus, all the initiatives taken under COE will be refered as the initiatives taken by DBIT to promote & contribute towards OSS community.

Activities:

  • Short term goals allottment to the COE members.
  • Role (Admin, Trainer , Developer, consultant etc..)based training on some specific OSS/tools.
  • Mastering OSS/tools used in IT & Comps engineering stream
  • Feeture specific comparartive study of properietery software/tool and OSS/tools
  • Establishment of OSS Club (Faculty & Students).
  • Survey of other institutions/colleges/schools to identify possible requirements/services
  • Projecting ourselves as a service providers
